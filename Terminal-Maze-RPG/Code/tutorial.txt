  Welcome to the Arcane Trials! All magic acolytes must complete a challenge of skill
        And resilience in order to prove themselves worthy of the title WIZARD!

  You now find yourself about to embark on the greatest challenge of your life.

  You must solve the mazeâ€¦

  Find all 8 power crystals and make your way to the inner chamber,
        And you will be able to call yourself WIZARD!

  Be careful! The maze is full of monsters. If you should perish, you will be revived
        At one of the resurrection stones scattered throughout the maze.

  Unfortunately, the maze has a mind of its own. Every time you revive, you will
        Awaken to find the entire maze has rearranged itself!

  To help you on your journey, the council has added 16 health pools around the maze
        Collecting them will increase both your current and maximum health.

  How many health pools you collect will add to our evaluation of your performance.

  Merely surving the maze will not be enough to earn the title of master!

  Good luck, and let your journey begin!





  Controls:

  w - move up     a - move left     s - move down     d - move right

  m - open or close larger map

  q - quit




  How to Read Map:

  The maze is divided into a 3 x 3 grid, with 8 regular chambers and a central chamber

  Each of the 8 regular sections is divided into a 2x2 grid of 4 pieces

  Each piece contains a 15x15 grid of tiles representing either walls or paths

  The regular screen will show you a single 15x15 piece and a first person view

  The map screen will show the 30x30 section, as well as an overview of the entire maze
